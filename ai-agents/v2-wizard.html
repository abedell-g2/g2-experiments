How much should the agent act on its own?
This determines the Autonomy Score threshold we apply to your results. Lower autonomy = more human control; higher = less oversight required.
Fully Autonomous
The agent researches, decides, and acts — you only review outcomes. Good for teams that want to scale without adding headcount.
Mostly Autonomous
Handles execution but surfaces key decisions for your approval. Best for teams wanting AI efficiency with human judgment on important calls.
Supervised
The agent drafts and recommends; humans approve before anything is sent. Ideal for regulated industries or teams new to AI agents.
