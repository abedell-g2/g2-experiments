How much should the agent act on its own?
This determines the Autonomy Score threshold in your results. Lower autonomy = more control; higher autonomy = less oversight required.
Fully Autonomous
The agent researches, decides, and acts — you only review outcomes, not every step. Good for teams that want to scale without adding headcount.
Mostly Autonomous
The agent handles execution but surfaces key decisions for your approval. Best for teams that want AI efficiency with human judgment on important calls.
Supervised
The agent drafts and recommends; humans approve and send. Ideal for regulated industries or teams new to AI agents who need confidence before scaling up.
